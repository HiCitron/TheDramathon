Refund policy

Refund policies vary slightly from event to event – please check the specific details on the entry form of the race you are entering. The following example refund policy shall otherwise be in place;

  1. Entrants wishing to withdraw must inform the organiser by email. Entry fees will be refunded or transferred as follows; up to (1 month prior to race date) 75% refund (less any Entry Central or SI fees), up to (2 weeks prior to race date) 50% refund (less any Entry Central fees). After (2 weeks prior to race date) - no refund.

  2. Due to the nature of sports events we are unable to make exceptions for injuries or pregnancy.

  3. Date of withdrawal is date of the email. Telephone or Facebook messages are not acceptable.

  4. If the event is cancelled for reasons beyond the organiser’s control, (e.g. adverse weather conditions, major incident etc.) the organisers will make every reasonable attempt to reschedule the event. Original entrants will have their entry automatically transferred unless they advise The Dramathon that they do not wish to transfer to the rescheduled event in writing (email to info@thedramathon) within 7 days of the original event date.

  5. In the event of cancellation, original entrants may, at the sole discretion of The Dramathon be offered an entry transfer of equivalent value to an alternative event in the companies’ portfolio.

  6. In all cases, The Dramathon reserves the right to charge a small administration fee (typically £5) to cover costs associated with card repayments and refund administration time.

  7. No refunds will be granted after one month has passed from the event date.

Changes to Entries

You may change your entry up to two weeks before the event, and this must be done in writing to the organiser with full details (info@thedramathon.com). This will be subject to any increases in entry fee, plus a £5 admin fee (which must be paid and received by The Dramathon prior to the changes being made).

Examples include change from a Solo Entry to a Relay Team Entry (where applicable) or change (with express permission of the Race Organiser) in the name and other details associated with an entry.
